Apple hosted its major fall event on September 9, 2025, under the tagline “Awe dropping”, unveiling the new iPhone 17 lineup and the ultra-thin iPhone Air. The iPhone 17 introduces the powerful A19 chip, upgraded 48 MP cameras, and a larger, brighter ProMotion display with Always-On functionality. iPhone Air was presented as Apple’s thinnest iPhone ever, featuring a lightweight, durable titanium chassis and performance on par with the Pro models.

Apple also introduced new Apple Watch models — Series 11 , SE 3 , and ultra 3 — with expanded health features such as hypertension monitoring and improved battery life. The AirPods Pro 3 received a refresh with enhanced noise cancellation, new sensors, and real-time translation. At the same event, iOS 26 was announced, featuring a new design language called “Liquid Glass” and multiple improvements to the overall user experience.
